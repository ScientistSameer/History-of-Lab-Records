Cybersecurity and Network Defense Laboratory

The lab currently has 24 researchers in total, including 6 senior researchers, 12 PhD scholars, and 6 research interns.

Currently managing 9 ongoing projects, with operational capacity up to 22 projects. Workload score is 7. The lab is available for new collaborations.

The lab collaborates with cybersecurity firms, government agencies, and academic institutions. Open for collaboration in Cybersecurity, Network Security, Cryptography, and Threat Analysis.
